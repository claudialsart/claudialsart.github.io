Claudia Ls Art

Italian-born oil painter based in London. My work explores space, emotion, and the silence in between.

What I Paint

I create what I cannot explain in words, emotions that echo like gravity, stillness that feels infinite. Through oil painting, I explore the quiet drama of space and the intimate turbulence of human feeling. My style moves between realism and abstraction, between cosmos and closeness. I call it abstract emotional realism, a visual language of tenderness, silence, and survival.

My Journey

I’m a self-taught artist. I didn’t follow a traditional path. I carved one. My art is born from solitude, deep reflection, and a personal transformation sparked by motherhood. I lost almost everything: my home, my studio, and my sense of direction. So I began again. Painting became a way to hold onto something real. Something I could control, shape, and pour myself into. It still is.
