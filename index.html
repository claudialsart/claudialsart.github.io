About Me

I am a self-taught artist whose work explores the intersection between emotion, memory, and visual storytelling. My recent artistic rebirth was born through motherhood — a deeply transformative experience that changed both how and why I create. My art now navigates themes of tenderness, chaos, and internal worlds.

I carry a profound love and fear of space — its vastness, its silence — which has led me to paint celestial elements as symbols of inner emotions. Through brushstrokes that blend realism and abstraction, I seek to give form to feelings that are often difficult to name.
